What is SpearSoft?
SpearSoft Technologies is a startup company providing Software Development, Software Quality Assurance, Robotics and Staffing & Recruiting based out of Hyderabad. SpearSoft provide IT / Software development and services and is working on multiple Robotics Research and Development projects and products. We have in-house design, rapid prototyping hardware and software development, DIY Robots, BoT, Satellite and manufacturing kit. We are committed to creating IT developers / professionals through our Workshops, Trainings and Advanced courses. Mission for IT Services / Robotics: Success is not set by talent alone. Mentoring, training and learning plays a very important role. Teaching and learning are set to transform rapidly and our mission at SpearSoft is to improve the preparation of students for productive functioning in the highly demanding domain and to strengthen the youth into Robotics and other technologies from all the parts of the country.
